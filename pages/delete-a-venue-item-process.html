Delete a Venue item process

 

The purpose: To delete a Venue item on the BagIT database.

 

How to: The User clicks on the Categories tab on the navigation bar and clicks the Venue tab. The User will click on the Delete button in the table row of the Venue item. The Delete confirmation prompt will be displayed, and the User will select OK or Cancel to confirm the deletion of the Venue item.
