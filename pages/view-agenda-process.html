View Agenda process

 

The purpose: To view the schedule agenda on the BagIT system.

 

How to: The User will click the ‘Schedule’ item in the navigation bar. The ‘Schedule’ page will be loaded, where the User will click on the Agenda button on the top right of the schedule home page.
