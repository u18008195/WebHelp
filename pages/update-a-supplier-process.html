Update a Supplier process

 

The purpose: To update a Supplier item to the BagIT database.

 

How to: The User clicks on the Supplier tab on the navigation bar and clicks the View Supplier link. The User will click on the Update button in the table row of the Supplier item. The Update Supplier form will be displayed, and the User will fill in the details of a Supplier.
