Modify of a Client process

 

The purpose: To update a Clients to the BagIT database.

 

How to: The User clicks on the Clients tab on the navigation bar. The User will click on the Update button in the table row of the Clients item. The Update Clients form will be displayed, and the User will fill in the details of a Clients.
