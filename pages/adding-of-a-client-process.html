Adding of a Client process

 

The purpose: To add a new Clients to the BagIT database.

 

How to: The User clicks on the Clients tab on the navigation bar. The User will click on the Add button on the top right of the Clients home page. The Add Clients form will be displayed, and the User will fill in the details of a Clients.
