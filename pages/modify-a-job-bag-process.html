Modify a Job Bag process

 

The purpose: To modify a Job Bag to the BagIT database.

 

How to: The User clicks on the Job Bag tab on the navigation bar. The User will click on the required Job Bag Card on the Job Bag page. The View Bag page will display. The User will click on the Modify button. The Modify Job Bag form will be displayed, and the User will fill in the details of a Job Bag.
