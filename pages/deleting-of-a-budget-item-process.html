Deleting of a Budget item process

 

The purpose: To delete a Budget from a Job Bag.

 

How to: The User clicks on the Job Bag tab on the navigation bar. The User will click on a Job Bag status. The View Bag page will display. The user clicks on the Budget button on the middle of the screen. The Budget form will be displayed, and the User will delete the Budget.
