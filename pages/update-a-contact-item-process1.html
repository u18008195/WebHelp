Update a Contact item process

 

The purpose: To update a Contact item in association with the Supplier to the BagIT database.

 

How to: The User clicks on the Contacts button in the Supplier table. The User will be directed to the Contacts home page and clicks on the Update button in the Contacts table. The Update Contact form will be displayed, and the User will fill in the details of a Supplier Contact.
