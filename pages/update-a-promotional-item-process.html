Update a Promotional item process

 

The purpose: To update a Promotional item to the BagIT database.

 

How to: The User clicks on the Categories tab on the navigation bar and clicks the Promotional items tab. The User will click on the Update button in the table row of the Promotional items. The Update Promotional items form will be displayed, and the User will fill in the details of a Promotional items.
